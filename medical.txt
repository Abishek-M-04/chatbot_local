Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects how the body processes blood sugar.
Symptoms include increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, and blurred vision.

Fever is a temporary increase in body temperature.
It is often caused by infection and may be accompanied by chills and sweating.

Asthma is a condition in which airways narrow and swell.
Symptoms include wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, and coughing.
